# CareAttend Logo Prompt Pack

Use these prompts with Gemini, OpenAI image generation, Midjourney, Adobe Firefly, or any professional logo generator.

## Brand Direction
CareAttend is an NHS-aligned healthcare technology app that predicts missed appointment risk and recommends ethical, explainable interventions for vulnerable patients. The logo should feel trustworthy, clinical, modern, accessible, and human-centred.

## Visual Requirements
- Use NHS-inspired blue as the main colour: #003087
- Secondary colours allowed: teal #00A499, light blue #41B6E6, white, charcoal
- Avoid red as a primary colour because it signals emergency/risk
- Must work as an app icon, website header logo, PDF report logo, and presentation logo
- Should be simple enough to recognise at 32px
- No copyrighted NHS logo, no official NHS lozenge, no misleading government branding
- Avoid generic heart-only or cross-only designs unless combined with appointment/attendance symbolism

## Prompt 1: Best Overall
Create a professional vector logo for "CareAttend", a UK healthcare AI app that helps GP practices reduce missed appointments for vulnerable patients. Combine three ideas: compassionate care, appointment attendance, and responsible AI. Use a clean icon with a calendar/checkmark subtly integrated with a protective care symbol. NHS-inspired blue (#003087), teal accent (#00A499), white background. Minimal, modern, accessible, trustworthy, clinical, scalable app icon. Include separate icon and wordmark. No official NHS logo. No photorealism. No mockup. Flat vector style.

## Prompt 2: App Icon Focus
Design a mobile app icon for "CareAttend". Icon should show a calendar page with a gentle checkmark and small human care motif, suggesting patients attending appointments. Use NHS-inspired blue background (#003087), white calendar, teal checkmark. Rounded square icon, clear at small size, modern healthcare SaaS style. No text inside icon. No official NHS logo. Flat vector, high contrast, accessible.

## Prompt 3: AI + Explainability Focus
Create a healthcare AI logo for "CareAttend" showing a simple shield/care shape with small connected nodes and a checkmark, symbolising safe explainable AI for appointment attendance. Use NHS-inspired blue, teal, and white. Professional UK healthtech brand, trustworthy, ethical, minimal vector mark with wordmark. Avoid robots, brains, stethoscopes, and official NHS branding.

## Prompt 4: Vulnerable Patient Support Focus
Create a warm but professional logo for "CareAttend", a health app supporting elderly and vulnerable patients to attend appointments. Icon concept: two abstract people inside a calendar outline with a checkmark. NHS-inspired blue and teal, clean sans-serif wordmark, accessible, calm, reliable. Flat vector logo, white background, no official NHS logo.

## Negative Prompt
Do not use the official NHS logo, government crest, stock medical cross alone, ambulance, emergency red, complex gradients, photorealistic mockups, tiny unreadable text, cartoon mascot, overly futuristic robot, busy background, or fake hospital branding.

## Best Final Logo Concept
Recommended: calendar + checkmark + care shield/person motif. This links directly to missed appointments, healthcare support, and safe decision-making. It is stronger than a generic heart/cross because it tells the exact CareAttend story.
